# CLAIM-0003: Charged-Lepton Koide Reproduction

## Statement

The charged-lepton pole masses in the configured benchmark define a Koide
quantity numerically close to `2/3`.

## Evidence Status

`RESULT-0005` exists for the charged-lepton reproduction benchmark, but this
claim remains `DRAFT` until the maintainer reviews the uncertainty-aware
interpretation and confirms the public wording.

## Scope

This claim is limited to:

- charged leptons only;
- one explicit PDG-backed dataset snapshot;
- reproduction of the Koide quantity, not explanation of mass generation.

## Caution

This claim must not be promoted from a numerically close reproduction result
into an explanatory statement. No cross-family generalization or new-physics
interpretation is licensed by this benchmark alone.
