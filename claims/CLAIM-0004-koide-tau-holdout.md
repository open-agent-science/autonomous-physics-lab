# CLAIM-0004: Historical Tau Holdout Benchmark

## Statement

Under the exact charged-lepton Koide assumption, electron and muon pole masses
predict a tau mass numerically close to the measured charged-lepton value.

## Evidence Status

`RESULT-0006` exists for the historical tau holdout benchmark, but this claim
remains `DRAFT` until the maintainer reviews the predictive framing and
confirms the non-explanatory wording.

## Scope

This claim is limited to:

- charged leptons only;
- one explicit PDG-backed dataset snapshot;
- a holdout prediction benchmark, not an explanation of mass generation.

## Caution

This claim must not be promoted from a numerically close holdout benchmark into
an explanatory statement. No cross-family generalization or deeper physical
interpretation is licensed by this benchmark alone.
