# Raw And Derived Policy

The raw ASCII artifact is committed unchanged and checksum-pinned.

The normalized YAML is an APL-authored structural transformation only. It
preserves all five source columns and records their direct, residual, derived,
or modeled provenance classes.

No `B_nu` to `B_lambda` conversion, interpolation, blackbody fit, peak
location, temperature selection, or residual metric is permitted in TASK-0801.
