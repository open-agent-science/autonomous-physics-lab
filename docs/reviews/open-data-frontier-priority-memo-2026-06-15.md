is source-to-row acquisition, not idea generation. Two frontier campaigns were,
on this date, nearest to a first promotable RESULT on **open data** and could be
driven in parallel on disjoint surfaces:

mean abandoning source-starved campaigns (Quantum, Atomic), which keep their own
bounded lanes in their campaign docs. Source selection follows the
[open-licensed-first preference order](../fresh-data-intake-protocol.md#source-selection-preference-order-open-licensed-first).
